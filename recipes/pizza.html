Pizza

Description

Pizza is one of the world's favorite food items. From delicious New York style, to Chicago style, you can't go wrong.

For this recipe we will be looking at a New York style recipe, and we will keep it simple with the flavor: cheese pizza! We will use ingredients that are fresh, easy to find, and affordable that you can cook at home with your family. We hope you enjoy the recipe as much as we do, let's get started!

Ingredients

  • 1 c. pizza suce
  • 8 oz. provolone cheese, thinly sliced
  • 2 c. shredded low-moisture whole-milk mozzarrella cheese
  • 6 tbsp. grated parmesan cheese
    • Ree's Homemade Pizza Dough
